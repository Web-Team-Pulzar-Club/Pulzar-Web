Who We Are

PULZAR is the first-ever academic society established at HNDIT Labuduwa, targeting a wide range of students island-wide to enhance academic activities with modern, innovative approaches.

Our mission is to provide students with opportunities to engage in cutting-edge technology, practical learning, and collaborative projects, ensuring they are well-prepared to excel in the evolving IT landscape.
